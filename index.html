A letter to you

Happy birthday Babyguirl

Hi. Jayden here. Happy birthday to you. You're 19 now. I wish I was 19, I feel like I’m growing old but at least I get to grow with you, so I guess it’s all good. I just wanted to say that I’m sorry if I sounded harsh yesterday because I found out that the plans I made for this month might not happen. but don’t worry about it. it’s your birthday so we're gonna celebrate it. Karaoke? Fufu? A cake? Pictures in the park? I promise this time I would give you suggestions.

I was writing this while thinking. It’s already a year? I don’t know but it feels like yesterday we started dating. I wish I could go back to seeing the girl with the orange hair and boots outside that everyone was gawking at. The day we walked to Surbiton station from Seething Wells. The day we rode the boat on the riverside, and I taught you how to ride the boat. Isabella plantation and the McDonald trip in Aldi or something. I feel like I took all that for granted and I want to go back ngl. When we were "just friends" you know.

Also, it’s been a year, and I feel like I’ve known you for like 30 years or something (the time isn't specific). I just feel like I’ve known you for a while. Mainly because I’ve changed a lot. Sometimes I wonder if I would be the person that I am without you by my side. Someone taught me gratitude. I don’t really know exactly what that means but I am grateful for who I am and who I’m going to become. Someone decided to become a baby and started throwing tantrums, so I learnt how to be a little more patient with people. honestly, I don’t really think I’m patient but i had a customer at work tell me that I’m very patient even with all the pressure in the store. I guess it’s a testament to how well you've raised me lol. Someone taught me to wrestle with things I don’t really understand. Don’t be afraid to ask questions and don’t be afraid to believe and understand what you really believe. Maybe you didn’t explicitly say these things but being with you has made me question stuff I used to believe in. I ask questions not because I want to dismiss something but understand things from another perspective. which carries me here. Someone taught me how to be more compassionate and understand other people's perspectives. I used to think there's only yes and no to every question. but I started to think that maybe is actually nuance. I’m trying to be smart and smooth with words but all I’m trying to say is it doesn’t always have to be white or black. it could be grey? idk. hot and cold? maybe warm instead yk or lukewarm or something I don’t think I’m making sense but maybe logic isn’t the only thing that matters. I’m trying to unlearn that but there was a time where I used to think that logic solves every problem and there's nothing like nuance. Oh, also someone helped me find confidence in myself. I feel better taking pictures now so thank you for dressing me up and giving me fit recommendations. I hope you dress me up more in the future to come.

Also, I find it really impressive how you're working two jobs. I swear I would pass out. you really are a strong woman. you're pretty and funny as shi. You have nice fashion sense, and you are a great stylist. My stylist. Your cooking is great and I’m not even joking. Your mango and sticky rice? Omdsss. Even your buldak tastes like a 5-star meal I aint even joking. You say you don’t like kids but you're really good with them I won’t lie. I like how you're careful and caring. I like how you try to understand me every time I come to you with problems instead of using them against me and I really appreciate it genuinely. And I really appreciate you taking care of me and I promise to strive to take care of you too and treat you well.

Alot of yappatronics but everything I said can be summarised in one paragraph. First of all, change all the likes to love because I really love you soo much and I’m really grateful for you. You always ask me if I would love you forever and honestly, I can’t imagine life with anyone else in your place. I love you dearly and I wish you a very happy birthday. I was going through this letter, and I realised that there’s a lot of “I” s. Which is weird cause its supposed to be a letter for you on your birthday. But when I think about it, it makes sense. I don’t think its selfish cause to me, everything I have learnt and experienced is an extension of who you are as a person. Its proof of your 19th year in my life. And so yeah, although it’s “I” s and stuff it’s more like “I am this and that because you exist in my life”. I pray that you get 19 more years of joy, grace, success, happiness, money, strength, knowledge, wisdom, everything good life has to offer. I love you very much and I can’t wait to spend more years with you.

Love,
Jayden Himothy
